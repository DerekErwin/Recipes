INGREDIENTS:

  1. 1 pound lean ground beef
  2. 1 cup chopped onion
  3. 1 package McCormick Chili Seasoning Mix
  4. 1 (15-ounce) can kidney beans, drained
  5. 1 (14.5-ounce) can diced tomatoes, undrained
  6. 1 (8-ounce) can tomato sauce

DIRECTIONS:

  1. Place ground beef and onion in a large skillet and saute over medium-high heat for 5 minutes, or until no longer pink. Drain fat.
  2. Mix in the chili seasoning mix, canned tomatoes, and tomato sauce. Bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to low and cover the pot. Simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally to keep the bottom from scorching.
  3. Spoon over bowls and top with shredded cheese, sour cream, and chopped leeks, if desired. Serve and enjoy!
